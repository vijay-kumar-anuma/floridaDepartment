If you represent an insurance company as its designated agent for service of process, please review the information below and login to access the files served to you. Pursuant to § 624.307(9), Florida Statutes (2015), Upon receiving service of legal process issued in any civil action or proceeding in this state against any regulated person required to appoint the Chief Financial Officer as its attorney to receive service of all legal process, the Chief Financial Officer, as attorney, may, in lieu of sending the process by registered or certified mail, send the process by any other verifiable means to the person last designated by the regulated person to receive the process. Pursuant to records and documents filed with the Department of Financial Services or the Office of Insurance Regulation, you are designated as the person to whom legal process for one or more companies or entities should be forwarded. We are forwarding and making images of authorized documents immediately available to you in your secured account, electronically through LSOP. This electronic notification is available to you as a designated agent of an insurer, company, or entity that is statutorily required to designate the Florida Chief Financial Officer as its Statutory Registered Agent for Service of Process; this notification is not available on the web for public viewing. As such, you will be the recipient of this notification and will control the access to the images relating to the company(ies) you represent. You have the ability to download the file or files that contain an image of each suit, and you are expected to download all information on the day you are notified and/or the files are made available to you in your secured account. The LSOP system will document the date and time you download each file. Failure to promptly download the documents could result in serious legal and financial consequences including court delays and/or court sanctions including, but not limited to, default judgments and contempt charges. We make every effort to assure you are promptly notified regarding service, so please keep your email addresses and user accounts current and ensure that your security settings allow for receipt of LSOP notifications. You acknowledge and understand that email messages are sometimes rejected or spammed by a recipient’s security settings. Even without receipt of an email notification, you should log into the LSOP system frequently to confirm whether LSOP has provided service documents to you for download. We recommend each designated agent log in and review the agent’s account at least weekly. To download your companies documents please confirm that you understand your responsibility and login to the system now.
